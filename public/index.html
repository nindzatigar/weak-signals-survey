Weak Signals Survey

This is a short conversation about the small, easy to miss shifts in how AI is changing the production and consumption of news right now, the kind of thing that's easy to notice in passing but rarely gets asked about directly. It's built on two reports, AI in Journalism Futures 2024 and AI in Journalism Futures 2025, and uses the Use Cases database from JournalismAI as a foundation, checking what you share against more than 100 documented examples from newsrooms worldwide. The goal is to fold what you notice into a live, crowdsourced map of where journalism and AI are actually heading, grounded in real experience rather than speculation. It takes no longer than 20 to 30 minutes, usually less, and stays anonymous: your name is optional, your role isn't. To start, tell me your role in the newsroom and roughly how long you've been doing this kind of work.
